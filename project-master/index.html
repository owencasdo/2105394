Vulture Culture

An online subculture and form of hobbyist taxidermy based on the collection and preservation of animal remains.

Vulture Culture

About the history: Vulture culture originated over the internet during the early 2010's, where people decided to make a subculture of the taxedermy hobby dedicated to the preservation of bones

Collections

Vulture culture can include many things that aren't bones, such as insects and feathers, people may choose to display them as a collection or in an artistic way.
